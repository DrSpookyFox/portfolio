We Boulder'n?

Owner and Lead Developer, Ongoing Project

A web app to help climbers have a proper bouldering session

  • Built with Vite, React, Docker, Bootstap, HTML, CSS
  • One of the hardest things about rock climbing (besides gravity) is finding the right conditions to climb in. When it comes to bouldering it's especially important to have the right temps to climb the boulder
  • With my 6 years of knowledge of boulders in the New York / New England area, I designed an app that would show you which boulders are available to climb based on your preferences and the current weather in that area
  • The hardest challenge for me was managing state across the whole app. I frequently had to go back to my diagram where the state lived for each component. (I look forward to working with technologies with state management, but for this project I wanted to learn the hard way.) Also the sliders for the preferences page were weirdly hard for me to navigate.
  • Future considerations would be to develop into a web app, build log-in page for users so they can record boulders they climbed, take that data to determine what temperatures are best for certain climbs, and lastly incorporate sun direction to recommendations of boulders to climb
