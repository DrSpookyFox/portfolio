We Boulder'n?

A bouldering web app to help climbers have a proper session

  • Built with Vite, React, Docker, Bootstap, HTML, CSS
  • One of the hardest things about rock climbing (besides gravity) is finding the right conditions to climb in. When it comes to bouldering it's especially important to have the right temps to get the send (aka climb the boulder)
  • I used my 6 years of knowledge of boulders in New York/New England and designed an app that would show you which boulders are available to climb based on your preferences and the current weather in that area
  • The hardest challenege for me was managing state across the whole web app. I frequently had to go back to a map I drew of where state lived for each component. I look forward to working with technologies with state managment, but for this project I wanted to learn the hard way. Also the sliders for the preferences page were weirdly hard for me to navigate.
  • Future considerations would be to develop into a web app, build login page for users so they can record boulders they climbed, take that data to determine what temperatures are best for certain climbs, and lastly incorporate sun direction to recommendations of boulders to climb
